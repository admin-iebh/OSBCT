About this edition

A searchable, cross-referenced Unicode corpus of the Sixth Buddhist Council (Chaṭṭhasaṅgīti) recension of the Pāḷi Tipiṭaka, together with its Aṭṭhakathā (commentaries) and Ṭīkā (subcommentaries) — 118 volumes in all.
